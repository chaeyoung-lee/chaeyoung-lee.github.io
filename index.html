About me

I am a first year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Stanford co-advised by Professors Zerina Kapetanovic and Sara Achour. My research interest is in energy-efficient machine learning and mobile embedded systems.

I received a B.S. in Electrical Engineering & Computer Science at Yale where I was advised by Professor Lin Zhong. My research was on 5G Cloud RAN.
